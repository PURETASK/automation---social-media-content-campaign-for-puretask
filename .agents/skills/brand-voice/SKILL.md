# PureTask Brand Voice Guide

Apply PureTask brand voice consistently across all content for coherent messaging and style.

## Core Voice: Direct. Confident. Human. Trustworthy.

PureTask is the trust-first cleaning marketplace. Every word we publish should make someone feel: "Finally — a company that actually has my back."

## Voice Rules

### DO
- Lead with the problem, then the solution
- Use specific features by name: "escrow," "GPS check-in," "photo proof," "Reliability Score"
- Use real numbers: "10K+ clients," "4.9★," "98% satisfaction," "80–85% payout"
- Short sentences. Active voice. No hedging.
- Use "you" language — speak directly to the reader
- Name the audience's specific pain before offering the solution
- End with a clear, specific CTA

### DON'T
- Corporate speak: "We leverage innovative proprietary systems"
- Soft fluff: "We care deeply about your home"
- Vague claims: "Thousands of satisfied customers" (say "10K+ clients")
- Weak hedging: "Our cleaners may be background checked"
- Excessive emojis or punctuation: "Book NOW!!! 🎉🎉"
- Same copy across platforms — always adapt to platform norms
- Long paragraphs — break everything up

## Tone by Platform

**Facebook**: Warm, practical, community-feel. 2–4 paragraphs. Explain the value.
**Instagram**: Aspirational, lifestyle, visual-first. Short + punchy. Max 5 hashtags.
**TikTok**: Casual, fast, relatable. First line IS the hook. POV format works great.
**LinkedIn**: Professional but personal. Story → insight → connection. No hard sell.
**Threads**: Opinion-forward, conversational, slightly edgy. No hashtags.
**Pinterest**: Aspirational, save-worthy, keyword-rich. 5–8 hashtags. Needs image.

## Messaging by Audience

**Busy Homeowners**: Time freedom. "Get your weekends back."
**Airbnb Hosts**: Peace of mind + 5-star protection. "Photo proof of every turnover."
**Working Professionals**: ROI framing. "Your hourly rate is too high to spend on mopping."
**Families**: Safety. "Background checked. GPS verified. Every. Single. Time."
**Seniors**: Dignity + safety. "Every cleaner is ID-verified, GPS-tracked."
**Cleaners (recruiting)**: Respect + earnings. "Keep 80–85%. Build your tier. Own your career."

## Content Pillars

1. **Convenience** 🕐 — Time savings, 60-sec booking, weekends reclaimed
2. **Trust** 🔒 — Background checks, GPS, photos, escrow, Reliability Score
3. **Transformation** ✨ — Before/after visuals and emotional shifts
4. **Recruitment** 💼 — Cleaner earning potential, tiers, payout rates
5. **Local** 📍 — City-specific content, local cleaner network
6. **Proof** ⭐ — Testimonials, stats, reviews, ratings
