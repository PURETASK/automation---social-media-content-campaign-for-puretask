# PureTask Image Generation Skill

Generates HD social media images for PureTask content drafts using DALL-E 3.

## Brand Standards Applied Automatically
- Clean, minimal, professional photography style
- White/light gray dominant backgrounds
- Warm natural lighting
- Aspirational lifestyle photography
- No fake urgency, no clutter, no stock-photo feel
- Teal accent color: #0099FF where appropriate

## Output
- 1792x1024 (landscape/Facebook/LinkedIn)
- 1024x1024 (square/Instagram)
- HD quality, natural style
- Saved to puretask-assets/ and uploaded to CDN
