## TWO AUDIENCE SEGMENTS

### Segment A: Seniors Booking for Themselves (65+)
- **Primary motivation:** Independence + dignity. They want to stay in their home.
- **Fear:** Strangers in their home who aren't vetted.
- **Trust signals that matter:** Background checks, ID verification, GPS tracking, same cleaner option.
- **Tone:** Warm, respectful, reassuring. Never condescending. Never "old people" framing.
- **Best platforms:** Facebook (primary), Pinterest (secondary)
- **Best posting time:** Weekdays 10am-12pm PT

### Segment B: Adult Children Booking for Parents (35-55)
- **Primary motivation:** Peace of mind. They want mom/dad safe.
- **Fear:** Unreliable cleaner, no-shows, unsafe stranger in parent's home.
- **Trust signals that matter:** Same cleaner consistency, criminal background check, GPS arrival notification.
- **Tone:** Empathetic, practical, solution-focused. "You can't always be there — but PureTask can."
- **Best platforms:** Facebook (primary), Instagram (secondary), LinkedIn (tertiary)
- **Best posting time:** Evenings 6pm-8pm PT (when adult children are home from work)

## CONTENT DO'S AND DON'TS

### ✅ DO:
- Show warm, intergenerational scenes (adult child on phone with parent, family in clean home)
- Reference same-cleaner consistency as a core feature — this is #1 for seniors
- Mention GPS notifications — adult children love this ("know when they arrive + leave")
- Use warm, bright imagery — never dark or clinical
- Use larger, readable text in any graphic overlays
- Show clean, familiar home environments (not ultra-modern — should feel like their home)
- Mention background checks + ID verification prominently
- Reference the Reliability Score as a trust mechanism
- Include real stats: 4.9★, 2,400+ verified cleaners, 98% satisfaction

### ❌ DON'T:
- Use the word "elderly" — use "parents", "seniors", or just show the scenario
- Show frail or helpless imagery — show capable, dignified people
- Use small text or busy layouts in graphics
- Make it feel like an "old person" ad — make it feel like a caring service
- Reference price as a motivator — seniors prioritize trust over cost
- Use TikTok as primary platform (55+ is barely on TikTok)
- Use fast-paced video formats — 45-60 second paced videos preferred

## VISUAL STANDARDS FOR SENIORS CONTENT

**Photography style:**
- Warm, natural lighting (not the cool blue-white we use for general content)
- Familiar home settings — not ultra-modern. Think lived-in but clean.
- Intergenerational if possible: show the relationship, not just the clean home
- Professional cleaner should look warm, approachable, and professional
- If showing seniors: dignified, active, capable — not frail or dependent

**Color guidance:**
- Still use PureTask blue (#0099FF) for accents and CTA elements
- Add warm cream/beige tones to feel less clinical
- Green checkmarks for trust signals (same as general brand)

**Video guidance:**
- Slower pacing — 45-60 seconds preferred
- Avatar or VO: use avatar for Facebook/YouTube (more personal)
- Script should feel like a caring recommendation from a family member
- Always show GPS notification and background check as reassurance
