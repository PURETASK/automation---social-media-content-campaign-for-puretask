## Core Message

PureTask is launching something the cleaning industry has never seen. The people who join first don't just get a cleaning service — they get locked pricing, a direct line to the founding team, and the chance to shape what this platform becomes. The founding member window doesn't stay open forever. This is the ground floor.

---

## The Problem We're Solving

Early adopters have no reason to trust a new platform — they've seen cleaning apps come and go. The founding member concept flips this:
- Instead of asking for trust, we offer tangible perks for early commitment
- Instead of being a passive user, they become part of the origin story
- Instead of waiting, there's a clear reason to act NOW

---

## Audience Profile

**Who they are:**
- Age: 28–55
- Homeowners or renters who value their time and home environment
- Have disposable income — price isn't the primary barrier
- Early adopter personality — like being first, like exclusive access
- Likely already use (or have used) a cleaning service
- Frustrated with inconsistency from current providers

**What they feel:**
- Curious — "what makes this different from every other cleaning app?"
- Skeptical — "I've heard this before"
- Excited (once they see the features) — "this is actually different"
- Fear of missing out — "if this is real, I want locked pricing before it goes up"

**What they respond to:**
- Exclusivity — limited founding member spots
- Tangible perks — locked pricing, direct access, input on features
- The story — being part of building something new
- Social proof — others are joining, don't be left out
- Spring timing — fresh start, new season, new standard

---

## Emotion Arc (Week by Week)

### Week 1: CURIOSITY
**Theme:** "Something new is coming. It's different."
**Job:** Introduce PureTask as a vision, not a product. Make them lean in.
**Tone:** Warm, intriguing, confident
**Hook style:** "The cleaning industry hasn't changed in 20 years. We changed it."

### Week 2: DESIRE
**Theme:** "Here's exactly what founding members get."
**Job:** Make the perks concrete and real. This isn't vague — it's specific value.
**Tone:** Clear, exciting, generous
**Hook style:** "Founding members get things regular users won't."

### Week 3: FOMO
**Theme:** "The community is forming without you."
**Job:** Social momentum — others are joining, the window is real, don't be the person who waited.
**Tone:** Warm urgency, community pull
**Hook style:** "Your neighbors are already in. Here's what they know."

### Week 4: URGENCY
**Theme:** "Last call. Founding member pricing ends soon."
**Job:** Final conversion push. Clear deadline energy. This offer is real and closing.
**Tone:** Direct, urgent but not pushy
**Hook style:** "Founding member pricing closes [soon]. This is the last week."

## Visual Identity

### Art Style
Premium, aspirational, warm. Like an exclusive launch invitation. Spring palette — soft greens, warm whites, morning light. PureTask blue #0099FF as accent. Clean homes, bright windows, fresh start feeling. NOT corporate — intimate, personal, and exclusive.

### Color Palette
- Primary: PureTask Blue #0099FF
- Week 1: Soft spring greens and warm white — possibility and new beginnings
- Week 2: Gold and blue — premium, exclusive, value
- Week 3: Warm amber and cream — community, belonging, warmth
- Week 4: Deep blue with urgency red accent — close of window, action required

### Image Direction Per Week

**Week 1 — Vision:**
A beautiful, spotlessly clean home bathed in spring morning light. No people — just the space. The feeling of possibility. A fresh start. Flowers on the windowsill. Soft spring light pouring in. PureTask blue #0099FF accent subtle in the decor.

**Week 2 — Perks:**
A homeowner (30s, relaxed, professional) sitting in a clean bright living room, looking at their phone showing a "Founding Member" badge or locked pricing screen. Satisfied, exclusive, insider energy. Warm afternoon light. PureTask blue #0099FF on phone screen.

**Week 3 — Community:**
2–3 diverse homeowners/families shown separately but in similar warm clean home settings — visual suggestion of a community forming. Warm, bright, belonging. Spring light throughout. PureTask blue #0099FF elements in each scene.

**Week 4 — Urgency:**
A clean home at golden hour — the light is beautiful but clearly fading. A phone screen showing "Founding Member Window Closing." Tasteful urgency — not alarming, just real. PureTask blue #0099FF accent.

### Every Image Must Have
- Spring light — soft, warm, morning or golden hour
- A spotlessly clean home environment
- PureTask blue #0099FF element
- Premium, aspirational feel — never cheap or salesy
- Real people or real spaces — never stock photo energy
