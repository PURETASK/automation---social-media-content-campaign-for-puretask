## Core Message

Every cleaning company tells you to trust them. None of them can show you anything. PureTask doesn't ask you to trust us blindly — we give you GPS arrival data, timestamped before & after photos of every room, a Reliability Score built from real job history, and escrow payment that you control. You see the proof. You decide. Then you pay.

---

## The Problem We're Solving

The #1 barrier to booking a cleaning service online is trust. Specifically:
- "I don't know who this person is"
- "I have no way to verify they actually showed up"
- "I have no proof they did the work"
- "What if something goes wrong — I have no recourse"
- "I've been burned before"

PureTask has a specific technology answer to every single one of these fears. This campaign makes each answer visible, tangible, and credible.

---

## Audience Profile

**Who they are:**
- Age: 30–65
- Have either had a bad experience with a cleaning service OR are booking for the first time and cautious
- Adult children (35–55) managing care for aging parents — heightened trust requirements
- Families with children at home — extra cautious about strangers
- Working professionals who value proof over promises

**What they feel:**
- Skeptical — "every company says they're trustworthy"
- Anxious — "I'm letting a stranger into my home"
- Burned — "the last service disappointed me"
- Wanting to be convinced — "if you can actually show me, I'll book"

**What they respond to:**
- Specific technology (not vague claims)
- Real scenarios (not abstract benefits)
- Control — they want to be in charge of the process
- Proof they can verify themselves (GPS data, photos)
- The removal of risk (escrow means they don't pay until satisfied)

## Emotion Arc (Week by Week)

### Week 1: SKEPTICISM VALIDATED
**Theme:** "You're right not to trust — the industry earned that skepticism."
**Job:** Validate their caution. Don't argue with it. Stand with them against the problem.
**Tone:** Empathetic, honest, slightly indignant on their behalf
**Hook style:** "You're not being paranoid. The cleaning industry has a trust problem."

### Week 2: GPS
**Theme:** "You know the exact minute they arrived. Not approximately — exactly."
**Job:** Make GPS check-in feel real, specific, and genuinely useful. Not a feature — a feeling of control.
**Tone:** Reassuring, specific, calm confidence
**Hook style:** "9:02am. That's when your cleaner arrived. You know because PureTask told you."

### Week 3: PHOTO PROOF
**Theme:** "You see every room before you release a single dollar."
**Job:** Make before & after photos feel like the new minimum standard — not a bonus feature.
**Tone:** Empowering, visual, concrete
**Hook style:** "Before your next guest walks in, you've already seen every room."

### Week 4: ESCROW
**Theme:** "You're in control of payment. Not us. Not them. You."
**Job:** Flip the power dynamic on payment. Escrow = client holds all the cards.
**Tone:** Empowering, bold, definitive
**Hook style:** "You don't pay until you approve. That's not a policy. That's the system."

## Visual Identity

### Art Style
Clean, precise, tech-forward but warm. Technology as the trust signal. Phone screens showing real PureTask features — GPS maps, photo proof screens, escrow confirmation. Real homeowners reacting with genuine relief. PureTask blue #0099FF as the primary trust color throughout.

### Color Palette
- Primary: PureTask Blue #0099FF
- Week 1: Muted grey/amber — uncertainty, caution, the "before trust" state
- Week 2: Blue and white — precision, data, GPS clarity
- Week 3: Warm white and green — visual proof, the "I can see it" feeling
- Week 4: Deep blue and gold — control, power, satisfaction

### Image Direction Per Week

**Week 1 — Skepticism:**
A homeowner (30s–40s) looking uncertain before booking — maybe at their phone with a questioning expression. The "who is this person?" moment. Muted tones. Honest, not dramatic. The real anxiety of letting a stranger in.

**Week 2 — GPS:**
A phone screen clearly showing "Your cleaner arrived at 9:02am — GPS confirmed" notification. A homeowner (or adult child) seeing this notification with visible relief. PureTask blue #0099FF on the phone screen. Real moment, genuine emotion.

**Week 3 — Photos:**
Side-by-side before & after of a specific room (kitchen or bathroom). A homeowner reviewing these photos on their phone with a satisfied, relieved expression. The room transformation is dramatic. PureTask blue #0099FF accent.

**Week 4 — Escrow:**
A phone screen showing "Approve payment?" with a clear "Approve" button. A homeowner confidently tapping approve with a satisfied expression. Empowered, in control, decisive. PureTask blue #0099FF dominant on screen.

### Every Image Must Have
- A real PureTask feature shown in use (GPS, photos, escrow screen)
- A genuine human reaction (relief, satisfaction, confidence)
- PureTask blue #0099FF on the technology element
- Specific detail — not vague (show the actual notification, the actual photo, the actual screen)
- Never abstract — always concrete and real

✅ Reliability Score 0–100 — built from real GPS and job data, not just star ratings

✅ ID verification — real person, real profile
✅ Cleaner no-show (30+ min late) → full refund + $50 bonus to client
✅ Free cancellation with 48+ hours notice
✅ 2 grace cancellations per client for emergencies
