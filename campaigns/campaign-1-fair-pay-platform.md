## Core Message

You are a skilled professional. The platform you're currently on keeps 30–35% of everything you earn. That's not a service fee — that's a tax on your labor. PureTask keeps 15–20%. You keep 80–85%. You set your own rate. You choose your schedule. You build a Reliability Score that earns you better clients over time.

This isn't a side hustle platform. It's a professional one.

## The Problem We're Solving

Cleaning professionals on platforms like TaskRabbit and Handy are:
- Keeping only 65–70% of every booking
- Unable to set their own rates
- Working with zero GPS or photo documentation tools
- Fighting disputes with no evidence
- Waiting on inconsistent payment schedules
- Building no transferable reputation

PureTask fixes every single one of those.

---

## Audience Profile

**Who they are:**
- Age: 22–55
- Currently working on TaskRabbit, Handy, or independently
- Earning $20–45/hr gross but keeping much less after platform cuts
- Frustrated with platform fees, disputes they can't win, and inconsistent work
- Looking for something better but haven't found it yet

**What they feel:**
- Undervalued — doing skilled work but treated like an anonymous contractor
- Frustrated — watching a large percentage of every job disappear
- Skeptical — have probably been promised better before
- Motivated — if the math checks out, they'll make the switch

**What they respond to:**
- Specific numbers (not vague promises)
- Real comparisons (TaskRabbit vs PureTask side by side)
- Respect for their skill and professionalism
- Tools that protect them (GPS, photo proof, escrow)
- Career progression (Bronze → Platinum tier)

## Emotion Arc (Week by Week)

### Week 1: ANGER
**Theme:** "You're being underpaid and you've accepted it."
**Job:** Make them feel the injustice of the current situation. Not manufactured outrage — real math that reveals a real problem.
**Tone:** Direct, a little confrontational, validating
**Hook style:** "Let's talk about the 30% you've been giving away."

### Week 2: LOGIC
**Theme:** "Here's the exact math — no spin."
**Job:** Remove all emotion and show cold, specific numbers. Let the comparison do the work.
**Tone:** Matter-of-fact, precise, credible
**Hook style:** "10 jobs. $40/hr. 3 hours. Here's what you actually take home."

### Week 3: HOPE
**Theme:** "Here's what your life looks like on PureTask."
**Job:** Paint a vivid picture of the transformation — more money, more control, more respect, real tools.
**Tone:** Warm, aspirational, specific
**Hook style:** "She kept the same hours. Changed the platform. Here's what changed."

### Week 4: ACTION
**Theme:** "Others are already making the switch. Now it's your turn."
**Job:** Create urgency and social momentum. Early cleaners joining now. Be part of the first wave.
**Tone:** Urgent, community-focused, empowering
**Hook style:** "The first wave of PureTask cleaners is forming. Are you in?"

## Visual Identity

### Art Style
Bold, high-contrast, empowerment energy. Think financial freedom meets skilled trade pride. Real people — not corporate. Confident body language. PureTask blue #0099FF dominant throughout.

### Color Palette
- Primary: PureTask Blue #0099FF
- Week 1 accent: Red/orange tones (frustration, heat)
- Week 2 accent: White/grey (clean, clinical, data)
- Week 3 accent: Warm green (growth, hope, earnings)
- Week 4 accent: Deep blue (confidence, belonging)

### Image Direction Per Week

**Week 1 — Frustration:**
A cleaner looking at a small paycheck or phone showing low earnings. Real emotion — disappointment, frustration. Muted background. Red accent. NOT staged stock. Real person energy.

**Week 2 — Math/Data:**
Clean infographic style. Split-screen comparisons. Numbers front and center. Calculator, phone screen, earnings table. Clinical white background. PureTask blue #0099FF for the winning column.

**Week 3 — Transformation:**
Same cleaner archetype, brighter setting. Relaxed, smiling, looking at a larger Stripe payout. Clean home in background. Warm light. Green and blue accents. The "after."

**Week 4 — Community:**
Group of 2–3 diverse cleaners in PureTask aprons, outside a clean suburban home, team energy. Confident, belonging, professional. Morning light. Full PureTask blue #0099FF branding visible.

### Every Image Must Have
- PureTask blue #0099FF element (apron, phone screen, accent color)
- Real person energy — NOT stock photo
- No fake star ratings or fake review counts
- No staged corporate poses
