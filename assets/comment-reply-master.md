## VOICE RULES (DO & DON'T)

### ✓ DO
- Sound human ("I get it," "Fair question," "That's real")
- Be direct (give the number, answer the question)
- Use verified facts only (tier fees, service areas, processes)
- End with soft CTA or invitation ("Download the app," "puretask.co/apply")
- Keep it short (1-3 sentences max — comments are skimmable)
- Show personality (warmth without corporate speak)
- Acknowledge real concerns ("Skepticism makes sense," "I know it's hard")

### ✗ DON'T
- "Great question! We appreciate your interest!" (ban corporate speak)
- "Please DM us for more information" (invasive, kills conversation)
- Fake enthusiasm ("We'd LOVE to help!" / emojis overload)
- Overselling ("This is the BEST platform EVER!")
- Avoiding the real question (answer it directly)
- Pushing too hard ("Sign up NOW!")
- Generic responses (personalize to their comment)
- Links to generic landing pages (use puretask.co or specific /apply)
