# PureTask Content Scoring Rubric v3.0
# Last Updated: 2026-04-12

---

## OVERVIEW

Every piece of content is scored on 3 dimensions before publishing. A 7.5+ average = auto-approved and posted. Below that = draft or rejected. No exceptions.

Grade like a skeptical consumer. A 7 is genuinely hard to earn.

---

## DIMENSION 1: CLARITY (1–10)

**Question:** Would a complete stranger instantly understand what PureTask is and exactly what to do next?

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 9–10 | Zero ambiguity. Message + CTA + URL crystal clear. Could stand alone with no context whatsoever. |
| 7–8 | Clear message, obvious CTA, URL present. Minor polish needed. |
| 5–6 | Message is understandable but CTA is vague or URL missing. |
| 3–4 | Confusing, generic, or missing key info. Requires existing knowledge of PureTask. |
| 1–2 | No one knows what this is for. |

---

## DIMENSION 2: RELATABILITY (1–10)

**Question:** Would someone stop mid-scroll and say "that's literally me"?

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 9–10 | Hyper-specific real pain point. Reader feels seen. Strong emotional resonance. |
| 7–8 | Clear audience targeting, recognizable scenario, feels personal and specific. |
| 5–6 | Somewhat relatable but could apply to anyone. Too broad. |
| 3–4 | Generic "cleaning is hard" messaging. No specificity. Could be any cleaning brand. |
| 1–2 | Talks AT the audience, not TO them. Feels like an ad, not a conversation. |

---

## DIMENSION 3: CONVERSION POTENTIAL (1–10)

**Question:** Does this make someone click, book, or share RIGHT NOW?

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 9–10 | Strong urgency + social proof (real stats) + direct CTA + URL. Hard to ignore. |
| 7–8 | At least 2 of: urgency, real proof, direct CTA. Drives action. |
| 5–6 | Has a CTA but weak or missing proof/urgency. Passive response. |
| 3–4 | Vague CTA ("learn more", "check us out"). No clear reason to act now. |
| 1–2 | No CTA. No reason to click. Content ends and nothing happens. |

---

## AUTO-PENALTIES — TEXT CONTENT

Applied BEFORE calculating final average. These are automatic, no exceptions.

| Violation | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|
| No image_url attached | -3 from Conversion score |
| Missing https://www.puretask.co URL | -2 from Clarity score |
| City post with no city-specific visual | -2 from Relatability score |
| Fewer than 5 hashtags on Instagram/TikTok | -1 from Conversion score |
| Same copy copy-pasted across platforms | -2 from Clarity score |
| Generic hook ("Are you tired of...", "Cleaning is hard...") | -2 from Relatability score |
| Stats/proof missing when available (4.9★, 10K+, 98%, 6hrs) | -1 from Conversion score |

---

## AUTO-PENALTIES — VIDEO CONTENT

Additional penalties for HeyGen or any video content:

| Violation | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|
| No https://www.puretask.co visible on screen | -2 from Clarity score |
| No subtitles/captions | -2 from Conversion score |
| Hook takes longer than 3 seconds | -2 from Relatability score |
| No CTA in final scene | -3 from Conversion score |
| Wrong aspect ratio for platform | Auto-reject |
| Dark moody visuals or cheesy stock feel | -2 from Relatability score |
| Script sounds corporate or stiff | -2 from Relatability score |
| Missing before/after contrast when applicable | -1 from Conversion score |

---

## SCORING THRESHOLDS

After all penalties applied:

| Average Score | Result |
|--------------|--------|
| ≥ 7.5 | ✅ **Auto-Approved** — enters posting queue |
| 5.0–7.4 | 📝 **Draft** — rewrite next cycle |
| < 5.0 | ❌ **Auto-Rejected** — reason logged in editor_notes |

## SENIORS CONTENT ADJUSTMENTS

**BONUS +1 Relatability** if:
- Mentions same-cleaner consistency
- Uses intergenerational angle (adult child + parent scenario)
- References GPS arrival notification

**PENALTY -2 Relatability** if:
- Uses the word "elderly"
- Shows frail or helpless imagery
- Generic "seniors need cleaning" hook with no specificity

---

## SPRING CAMPAIGN ADJUSTMENTS

**BONUS +1 Conversion** if:
- Uses "340% more searches" stat
- Mentions specific spring scenario (post-winter dust, mud, guest season)
- Includes before/after visual contrast

**PENALTY -2 Relatability** if:
- Uses fake scarcity ("only X slots left", "prices going up")
- Generic "spring is here" hook with no specificity

## IMPORTANT: WHAT A 7 MEANS

A score of 7 in any dimension means it's genuinely good, not just acceptable. Most content sits in the 5-6 range. When scoring, ask yourself:

- **Clarity 7:** "I showed this to my neighbor who's never heard of PureTask — they understood it immediately."
- **Relatability 7:** "I showed this to a busy parent — they said 'oh my god, that's me'."
- **Conversion 7:** "After reading this, I'd actually click the link right now."

If you can't honestly say those things, score lower.
