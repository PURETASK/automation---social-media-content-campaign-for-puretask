## TRANSFORMATION VISUAL STRATEGY

PureTask is new — no real before/after photos yet. Use one of two approaches:

**Option A — Illustrated Split-Panel (recommended for static posts)**
- LEFT panel: messy/cluttered winter home, muted gray tones
- RIGHT panel: spotless spring-ready home, bright warm light, fresh flowers
- Clean dividing line between panels
- Professional illustration style, not photo-realistic
- Works better than real photos on social — cleaner and more visual

**Option B — Emotional Transformation Focus (recommended for video)**
- Don't show the mess at all
- Start: person looking stressed/overwhelmed
- End: person relaxed in sparkling clean home, windows open, spring light
- The transformation is emotional — from burden to relief
- Much more shareable than literal cleaning footage

## IMAGE VISUAL GUIDE

Spring images should feel:
- **Bright** — max natural light, open windows, sunshine
- **Fresh** — flowers on counters, green plants, light fabrics
- **Airy** — white and cream tones dominant
- **Transformative** — contrast between before/after always implied

Spring images should NEVER feel:
- Dark or moody (winter energy)
- Cluttered or overwhelming
- Corporate or sterile
- Generic stock photo

PureTask blue (#0099FF) appears as accent — not dominant. Spring uses warmer tones.
