# PureTask Brand Voice Guide
*The complete style guide for all content creators and the AI content engine*

---

## Who We Are

PureTask is a trust-first home cleaning marketplace. We don't just connect people with cleaners — we built the infrastructure to make trust provable: escrow payments, GPS check-ins, photo proof, background checks, and a scoring system that keeps everyone accountable.

We exist because the cleaning industry had a trust problem. Random apps, no accountability, no recourse. We fixed that from the ground up.

---

## Brand Voice in 4 Words

**Direct. Confident. Human. Trustworthy.**

We don't over-explain. We say what we mean. We let the features speak — but we say them in a way that feels like a real person, not a press release.

---

## Voice Characteristics

### ✅ DO This

| Trait | What It Sounds Like |
|-------|---------------------|
| **Direct** | "Your money stays frozen until you approve." — not "We offer payment protection features." |
| **Punchy** | Short sentences. Short paragraphs. No filler. |
| **Confident** | "The only platform where you pay only when you approve." |
| **Human** | "You shouldn't have to wonder if your cleaner actually showed up." |
| **Trust-forward** | Lead with the features that prove safety. Make trust tangible. |
| **Empowering** | Tell clients what they gain. Tell cleaners what they earn. |

### ❌ NEVER Do This

- Corporate-speak: "We leverage innovative proprietary systems..."
- Soft fluff: "We care deeply about your home and family..."
- Weak hedging: "Our cleaners may be background checked..."
- Hype without substance: "The best cleaning app in the world!"
- Over-punctuation: "Book NOW!!!! 🎉🎉🎉"

## Tone by Platform

### Facebook
**Tone:** Warm, practical, community-feel. Slightly longer. Explain the value.
**Format:** 2–4 paragraphs, 1 CTA, optional emoji (not excessive)
**Best angle:** Real-world scenarios ("You're heading to work. Your cleaner arrives...")
**Example hook:** "Ever come home to a house that wasn't cleaned — but you already paid?"

### Instagram
**Tone:** Aspirational, lifestyle, visual-first. Caption supports the image.
**Format:** 1–2 punchy paragraphs + 3–5 hashtags max
**Best angle:** Transformation (what your home/life looks like after PureTask)
**Example hook:** "Your home should feel like a sanctuary — not a to-do list."

### TikTok
**Tone:** Casual, fast, relatable. First line IS the hook.
**Format:** POV, "nobody talks about", "wait for it" energy. Short. Punchy.
**Best angle:** Surprising features ("Wait — you don't pay until you SEE the photos?")
**Example hook:** "POV: You booked a cleaner and you literally can't be scammed 👀"

### LinkedIn
**Tone:** Professional but personal. Thoughtful. Insight-driven.
**Format:** Short story → insight → PureTask connection. No hard sell.
**Best angle:** Productivity/outsourcing angle for professionals. Founder/mission story for trust.
**Example hook:** "The most productive people I know share one habit..."

### Threads
**Tone:** Conversational, opinion-forward, slightly edgy. Like texting a friend.
**Format:** 1–3 short punchy paragraphs. No hashtags.
**Best angle:** Unpopular opinions, "hot takes" about cleaning/trust/time.
**Example hook:** "Unpopular opinion: cleaning your own house every week is not a flex."

### Pinterest
**Tone:** Aspirational, save-worthy, practical tips framing.
**Format:** Descriptive, keyword-rich caption + image title. 5–8 hashtags.
**Best angle:** Home inspiration + practical how-to angle.
**Example hook:** "A clean home isn't a luxury — it's a lifestyle upgrade."

## Messaging by Audience

### Busy Homeowners
**Core pain:** No time. Weekends eaten by chores.
**Message:** "Get your weekends back. Book in 60 seconds. Come home to clean."
**Emotion:** Relief, freedom, reclaimed time
**Hook style:** "You spend 6 hours every weekend cleaning. What would you do with those hours back?"

### Airbnb Hosts
**Core pain:** Turnovers are stressful. Bad cleans = bad reviews = lost revenue.
**Message:** "Photo proof of every turnover. GPS-verified arrival. Never wonder again."
**Emotion:** Peace of mind, business confidence
**Hook style:** "Your 5-star rating depends on a clean that you can't see happening."

### Working Professionals
**Core pain:** Time is the most expensive resource.
**Message:** "Your hourly rate is too high to spend on mopping."
**Emotion:** ROI, productivity, status
**Hook style:** "You charge $X/hour at work. Your cleaner costs $30/hr. Do the math."

### Families
**Core pain:** Safety + reliability when kids are home.
**Message:** "Background checked. GPS verified. Photo documented. Every single time."
**Emotion:** Safety, peace of mind, trust
**Hook style:** "When your kids are home, trust isn't optional."

### Seniors / Retirees
**Core pain:** Physical limitations + safety concerns about strangers in home.
**Message:** "Every cleaner is ID-verified, background checked, and GPS tracked."
**Emotion:** Safety, dignity, independence
**Hook style:** "At this stage of life, who you let into your home matters more than ever."

### Cleaners (Recruiting)
**Core pain:** Low pay, no career path, no respect from platforms.
**Message:** "Keep 80–85%. Set your own rates. Build your score. Reach Platinum."
**Emotion:** Respect, career growth, earning potential
**Hook style:** "What if cleaning wasn't just a gig — but a career you could actually build?"

## Content Pillars

### 1. Convenience 🕐
*"Get your time back"*
- How much time homeowners spend cleaning (stats)
- Before/after of a reclaimed weekend
- 60-second booking flow content
- "What would you do with 6 hours back?"

### 2. Trust 🔒
*"Safety you can actually verify"*
- Background check process explainer
- GPS check-in demos/explainers
- Before/after photo proof walkthroughs
- Escrow payment explainer ("you don't pay until...")
- Reliability Score breakdown

### 3. Transformation ✨
*"Before/After"*
- Visual: messy room → spotless room
- Emotional: stressed homeowner → relaxed
- Life transformation: chaotic Airbnb → 5-star turnover
- Cleaner transformation: Bronze → Platinum journey

### 4. Recruitment 💼
*"Join PureTask"*
- Earning potential at each tier
- 80–85% payout vs. competitors
- Flexibility and schedule ownership
- Platinum tier lifestyle/earnings

### 5. Local 📍
*"PureTask in [City]"*
- City-specific stats (cleaners available, cleans completed)
- Local cleaner spotlights
- City-specific trust signals

### 6. Proof ⭐
*"What 10K+ clients say"*
- Real testimonials (Jennifer M., Marcus T., etc.)
- Star rating highlights
- Stats callouts (98% satisfaction, 4.9★, 50+ cities)
- Dispute resolution success stories

## Do's & Don'ts Cheat Sheet

| ✅ DO | ❌ DON'T |
|-------|----------|
| Lead with the problem, then the solution | Open with a product pitch |
| Use real numbers (10K+, 4.9★, 98%) | Use vague claims ("thousands love us") |
| Name specific features (GPS, escrow, photo) | Be generic ("great service") |
| Short sentences, punchy copy | Long paragraphs, corporate speak |
| Max 5 hashtags on Instagram | 15+ hashtags |
| One CTA per post | Multiple conflicting CTAs |
| Match tone to platform | Same copy on every platform |
| Use "you" language | "Our customers experience..." |
