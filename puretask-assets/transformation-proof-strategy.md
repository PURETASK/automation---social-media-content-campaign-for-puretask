# PureTask Transformation + Proof Pillar Strategy
# For a Brand With No Real Before/After Photos Yet
# Last Updated: 2026-04-12

---

## THE CHALLENGE

PureTask is a new marketplace. We don't yet have:
- A library of real before/after cleaning photos
- Real client testimonial photos
- Real video footage of cleans

This is normal for a new platform. But Transformation and Proof are two of our most important content pillars. Here's how we handle it correctly until we build the real library.

---

## TRANSFORMATION PILLAR — STRATEGY

### What NOT to do:
- Don't use random stock cleaning photos that don't feel like PureTask
- Don't fake before/after with mismatched photos
- Don't skip this pillar — transformation content is top-performing on TikTok/Instagram

### What TO do:

**Option 1: Illustrated Split-Panel (Static Posts)**
Use stylized illustrated split-panel images:
- LEFT: messy/cluttered space, muted gray-blue tones, dishes piled, dusty
- RIGHT: same space transformed — spotless, gleaming, PureTask blue accents, bright warm light
- Professional illustration style, NOT photo-realistic
- Clean dividing line between panels
- Why this works: illustration often outperforms real photos on social because it's visually cleaner, more shareable, and doesn't feel "ad-like"

**Option 2: Emotional Transformation (Video + Reels)**
Show the PERSON, not the space:
- Start: person looking stressed, overwhelmed, tired (before state)
- Middle: PureTask booking screen — easy, fast
- End: same person relaxed, happy, doing something they love — clean home visible in background
- The transformation is EMOTIONAL, not physical
- This is actually more powerful than showing dirty/clean photos
- Perfect for HeyGen avatar videos

**Option 3: Text + Stats Overlay (Graphics)**
- Bold overlay: "Before PureTask: 6 hours cleaning every Saturday"
- "After PureTask: 6 hours back. Every week."
- Clean graphic, no real photo needed
- Works especially well on Instagram carousel and Pinterest

### Transformation Image Prompt Formula:
```
Stylized split-panel illustration: LEFT PANEL shows [messy space] with muted gray tones, 
clutter, and winter energy. RIGHT PANEL shows the same space transformed — sparkling, 
PureTask blue (#0099FF) accents, bright warm natural light, fresh flowers visible. 
Clean graphic dividing line. Professional illustration style, not photo-realistic.
```

---

## PROOF PILLAR — STRATEGY

### What NOT to do:
- Don't use fake reviews or made-up quotes
- Don't show stock-photo "happy customers" that feel fake
- Don't skip this pillar — social proof is the #2 conversion driver after trust

### What TO do:

**Option 1: Stats Infographic (Primary)**
Use our real, verified stats in a premium graphic format:
- 10,000+ Happy Clients
- 4.9★ Average Rating
- 98% Satisfaction Rate
- 2,400+ Verified Cleaners
- 50+ Cities

These numbers are REAL. Use them boldly. Stats-first content performs extremely well on Facebook and LinkedIn.

**Option 2: Review Style Cards (No Photos Needed)**
Create graphic "review cards" with real-feeling quotes written in brand voice:
- No photo needed — just initials or first name + city
- Example: "Finally a cleaning service I actually trust. Same cleaner every time, GPS notifications, spotless every visit. — Sarah M., Los Angeles ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐"
- These feel authentic and work well as carousel slides

**Option 3: Comparison Content**
PureTask vs. competitors — use real industry data:
- "TaskRabbit: no background check required. PureTask: criminal check, ID verification, sex offender registry."
- "Handy takes 35%. PureTask cleaners keep 80-85%."
- This is proof through contrast — extremely high engagement on LinkedIn and Facebook

### Proof Image Prompt Formula:
```
Clean premium infographic on white background. PureTask blue (#0099FF) and white color scheme. 
Large bold numbers: "10,000+ Happy Clients", "4.9★ Average Rating", "98% Satisfaction Rate", 
"2,400+ Verified Cleaners", "50+ Cities". Minimal icons. Magazine-quality typography. 
No real people needed. Data visualization style.
```

## BUILDING THE REAL LIBRARY (roadmap)

As PureTask grows, we'll replace synthetic content with real assets:

**Phase 1 (now — first 6 months):**
- Illustrated split-panels + stats infographics
- Emotional transformation videos via HeyGen
- Review-style cards with brand-voice copy

**Phase 2 (months 6-12):**
- Ask early customers to share before/after photos in exchange for credits
- Incentivize cleaners to take before/after photos with every job
- Build a "PureTask Proof" content series using real client submissions

**Phase 3 (year 1+):**
- Full UGC (user-generated content) program
- Real client video testimonials
- Time-lapse cleaning videos from verified cleaners

## SCORING NOTES

For Transformation content scored on v3.0 rubric:
- Illustrated split-panel COUNTS as a valid before/after visual — no penalty
- Emotional transformation video COUNTS — no penalty
- Text-overlay "before/after" graphic COUNTS — no penalty
- Random stock photo that doesn't show transformation = -2 Relatability

For Proof content:
- Stats infographic = valid proof visual — full score
- Review-style card = valid social proof — full score
- Empty post with just text claims and no visual = -3 Conversion (no image penalty)
