## II. SENIORS CAMPAIGN — NEW INITIATIVE

### Market Opportunity
- 55+ spend $4.2B annually on home services
- Average senior household spends 2.4x more per clean than younger demographics
- 55+ is least-contested ad space in home services (competitors ignore it)
- Facebook 55+ has 58% daily usage — highest platform concentration for this demographic
- Repeat booking rate for seniors: 68% higher than general population
- Word-of-mouth within senior communities is extremely powerful (1 happy client = 5+ referrals)

### Two Distinct Audiences (with different CTAs)

**Audience A: Seniors (65+) booking for themselves**
- Primary motivation: Independence + dignity. Stay in their home longer.
- Fear: Strangers in home aren't vetted.
- Trust signals: Background checks, ID verification, same cleaner every visit.
- Best platforms: Facebook (primary), Pinterest (secondary)
- Posting time: Weekdays 10am-12pm PT

**Audience B: Adult Children (35-55) booking for parents**
- Primary motivation: Peace of mind. Want mom/dad safe.
- Fear: Unreliable cleaner, no-shows, unsafe stranger.
- Trust signals: Same cleaner consistency, GPS arrival notification, background check.
- Best platforms: Facebook (primary), Instagram (secondary), LinkedIn (tertiary)
- Posting time: Evenings 6pm-8pm PT (when they're home from work)

4. **Warm, dignified tone** — never use "elderly", never show frail imagery
5. **Intergenerational angles** (adult child + parent relationship) score highest for emotional resonance

## III. TRANSFORMATION/PROOF IMAGE STRATEGY

### The Problem
- Transformation pillar: 2 drafts (vs. 5-9 for other pillars)
- Proof pillar: 2 drafts (vs. 5-9 for other pillars)
- Both pillars need high-visual-impact imagery
- **Critical constraint:** PureTask is new. We don't have real before/after cleaning photos.

### The Solution: Stylized + Illustrated Visuals (Not Photo-Real)

**Transformation content** uses ONE of two approaches:
1. **Stylized split-panel illustration:** LEFT = messy/cluttered (muted gray tones, piled dishes, dusty). RIGHT = spotless/gleaming (bright, sparkling, fresh flowers, PureTask blue accents). Professional illustration style, not photo-realistic. This actually performs BETTER on social than real photos because it's cleaner and more visually striking.
2. **Emotional transformation focus:** Instead of dirty/clean photos, show the PERSON's relief/happiness after the clean. Lifestyle shot of them relaxing in their clean space. This is also excellent for conversion.

**Proof content** uses infographic design:
- Clean premium design on white background
- PureTask blue (#0099FF) and white color scheme
- Large bold stats: "10,000+ Happy Clients", "4.9★ Average Rating", "98% Satisfaction Rate", "2,400+ Verified Cleaners", "50+ Cities"
- Minimal icons, no real people, magazine-quality typography
- This is more trustworthy than photos anyway — stats beat anecdotes

### Image Generation Pipeline (Auto-Running)

**Deployment:** `generateImages` function runs hourly as scheduled automation.
- Processes 4 drafts per run (12s per image = no timeout)
- Prioritizes: Transformation > Proof > Trust > Convenience > Local > Recruitment > Seniors > Spring
- Fire-and-forget async: returns immediately, images populate as they complete
- Once all drafts have `image_url`, automation self-reports "All images complete" and pauses
