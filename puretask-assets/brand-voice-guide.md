# PureTask Brand Voice Guide
*The complete style guide for all content creators and the AI content engine*

---

## Who We Are

PureTask is a trust-first home cleaning marketplace. We don't just connect people with cleaners — we built the infrastructure to make trust provable: escrow payments, GPS check-ins, photo proof, background checks, and a scoring system that keeps everyone accountable.

We exist because the cleaning industry had a trust problem. Random apps, no accountability, no recourse. We fixed that from the ground up.

---

## Brand Voice in 4 Words

**Direct. Confident. Human. Trustworthy.**

We don't over-explain. We say what we mean. We let the features speak — but we say them in a way that feels like a real person, not a press release.
